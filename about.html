Who is JAM Project?

Thanks, Seokhun for your awesome band photo!

JAM Project (JAM meaning "Japanese Animationsong Makers"), is a Japan based rock band comprised of several singers who are popular in the Anison (Anime Song) genre of music. There are currently 5 active members (pictured from left to right):

  • Yoshiki Fukuyama
  • Hironobu Kageyama
  • Masami Okui
  • Hiroshi Kitadani
  • Masaaki Endoh

All members take part in writing and composing the band's songs, which have graced many Anime, video game, and sentai (special effects series-think Power Rangers) soundtracks. While JAM Project tours extensively in Asia, they have also performed in Europe, Latin America, and USA!
