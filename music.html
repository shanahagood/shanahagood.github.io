Music

JAM Project has released a total of 66 singles, 12 studio albums, and 5 original studio albums up-to-date. Jam Project's music, though predominantly rock, blends in elements of various other genres such as pop, folk, choral, and symphonic. They often sing about friendship, courage, love, overcoming obstacles, and sometimes, even giant robots (yes, robots, flying robots)! Songs are typically upbeat and catchy, but they throw a few ballads into the mix every now and then.
